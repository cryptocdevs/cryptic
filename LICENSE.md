**Solution:** Re-sign the `.ipa` file using your sideloading tool and re-install. Verify the developer profile is trusted under `Settings > General > VPN & Device Management`.

**Solution:** Check the script hub for updated versions. Ensure **Cryptic Executor** itself is on the latest build by enabling auto-update in the settings panel.

**Solution:** Go to `Settings > Apps > Cryptic Executor > Display over other apps` and toggle it on. For **Cryptic Executor Roblox Ios**, ensure the app has not been backgrounded by the system.
